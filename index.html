Our Products

Micro Accounting Software

Our micro accounting software is a simple and powerful tool that lets you manage your business finances with just two clicks. You can download it for free and start using it right away.

Curated Business Health Report

Our curated business health report is a comprehensive analysis of your business’s performance and potential. It gives you insights into your strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.

Micro or Nano Business Loans

Our micro or nano business loans are designed to support tiny entrepreneurs from rural, tier 2, and tier 3 regions of India. We offer loans ranging from Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 1.25 lakh, with flexible repayment options.
